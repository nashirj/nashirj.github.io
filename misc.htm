Climbr

During quarantine, I was unable to go to climbing gyms, so my housemate and I built a climbing wall in our backyard. I also created a web app to save routes that we set on the wall. To make the process of identifying holds on the wall automated, I experimented with using computer vision to detect holds on the wall.

NASA Micro-G NExT

In 2018-2019, Nashir worked on a NASA design project with fellow community college students. He and his team traveled to the Johnson Space Center in Houston, where he was the test conductor and taught NASA divers how to use the team's device for testing in the Neutral Buoyancy Laboratory.
