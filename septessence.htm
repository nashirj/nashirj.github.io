About the band

Septessence is a jazz septet, led by bassist Nashir Janmohamed, based in Orlando, FL. The members met while studying jazz at the University of Central Florida. Septessence plays a mix of original compositions by members of the band, as well as arrangements of other music, including jazz standards.

Members

Joshua Mercado (trumpet)


Josh is an Orlando-native jazz trumpet player who is currently attending the University of Central Florida. He has performed with many of the greatest jazz musicians playing around the world such as Joel Frahm, Clarence Penn, and Alex Claffy among others. He's also performed with numerous major artists who made hit songs that were on the billboard top 100 such as Shawn Stockman from "Boys II Men", Joey Fatone, and Chris Kirkpatrick from "Nsync". He's performed at Smalls Jazz Club in New York with some of his peers at UCF and is a reoccurring artist to many venues across Orlando.

Daniel Tenbusch (piano)


Originally from the Space Coast of Florida, Daniel began playing piano at a young age and took an early interest in jazz and popular music. In 2018 he graduated from Florida State University with a BM in jazz piano performance. After graduating, he moved to New Orleans, where he regularly worked with bands of many styles. In December 2018, Daniel alternated between working in New Orleands and working for drummer Les Demerle, completing two contracts for Royal Caribbean as part of Les's band. In May of 2020, Daniel took a short hiatus from music to hike the Appalachian trail, and since completion, has been based out of Florida. Daniel is currently pursuing a masters degree in music from the University of Central Florida where he teaches an undergraduate class in piano. He continues to work with his own trio as well as collaborate with artists throughout the Space Coast and Orlando area.

Nashir Janmohamed (bass, bandleader)


Originally from Los Angeles, California, Nashir Janmohamed is a graduate of UCLA, where he studied Jazz performance and composition. He has performed across the US (Catalina Bar and Grill, Jazz at LACMA, Central Avenue Jazz Festival, Vail Jazz Festival, Blue Whale, Caf√© 939, Whisky a Go Go, etc.) and has shared the stage with greats such as Kenny Burrell, Miguel Atwood-Ferguson, and John Clayton. Nashir has worked in various professional pit orchestras, and spent a summer playing in the house band on a cruise line. After graduating from UCLA, Nashir began studying computer science at the University of Central Florida, but still pursues his passion for music by writing original compositions and arranging jazz standards for Septessence and playing in various other groups in the Orlando area.

Jamesly Jean-Mary (drums)


Originally from Fort Lauderdale, Florida, Jamesly is a student at the University of Central Florida, where he is majoring in music. At UCF, he studied with Clarence Penn for two years, and is a member of the renowned Flying Horse Big Band. He went to Dillard High School of the Arts, where he was a member of the school jazz band, and won 1st place in the 2K18 Essentially Ellington National Competition.
