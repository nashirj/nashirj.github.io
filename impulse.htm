Impulse Trio

About the band

The Impulse Trio is a jazz trio, based in Orlando, FL. The members met while studying jazz at the University of Central Florida. The trio plays a mix of original compositions by members of the band, as well as arrangements of other music, including jazz standards.

Members

Noah Charles III (guitar)


Originally from Naples, Florida, Noah is a graduate of UCF, where he studied Jazz performance and composition. He has performed in venues across Florida; as a sideman, he has performed at Blue Bamboo, Timucua and the Sanford Jazz Festival. In 2022, Noah recieved 1st place in the Central Jazz Society Scholarship Competition. After graduating from UCF, Noah plans to work on new music, and pursue music full time - performing, composing, and teaching.

Nashir Janmohamed (bass)


Originally from Los Angeles, California, Nashir Janmohamed is a graduate of UCLA, where he studied Jazz performance and composition. He has performed across the US (Catalina Bar and Grill, Jazz at LACMA, Central Avenue Jazz Festival, Vail Jazz Festival, Blue Whale, Caf√© 939, Whisky a Go Go, etc.) and has shared the stage with greats such as Kenny Burrell, Miguel Atwood-Ferguson, and John Clayton. Nashir has worked in various professional pit orchestras, and spent a summer playing in the house band on a cruise line. After graduating from UCLA, Nashir began studying computer science at the University of Central Florida, but still pursues his passion for music by writing original compositions and arranging jazz standards for Septessence and playing in various other groups in the Orlando area.

James Fortune (drums)


Originally from Naples, Florida, James is a recent graduate of the University of Central Florida, where he majored in music. At UCF, he studied with Marty Morell for two years, and Clarence Penn for two years; he was also a member of the Flying Horse Big Band for two years. Since graduating from UCF, James has been performing in the Orlando area, and is currently working on his own music, as well as developing his skills as a producer.
