Thank you to our sponsors and supporters!

Interested in being a sponsor? Get in touch with us at quintessence.space@gmail.com!

Unbounded Media is media team that offers a variety of services, including corporate consultation, branding, digital marketing, photography and video, and much more. Their team is comprised of highly skilled individuals who aim to carry out marketing strategies for their clients through different social media platforms. Jonathan Phan, a member of Unbounded, joined Quintessence in their trip to Johnson Space Center and provided high quality video, photography, and insight into how best to use all the media gathered during the trip. It is clear to us that their high quality & personalized videography and photography services would be an asset to any company.

AstroAI offers a variety of products designed to make the daily lives of its customers easier and help them complete tasks more efficiently. AstroAI allowed us to work in their office space throughout the course of the project, and it is in large part thanks to them that we have made so much progress as a team.
